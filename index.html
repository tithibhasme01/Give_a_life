About Us

Charity for Golden Age, is charity in India directly benefitting over 1500,000 old once and also taking care of their hospitality , through more than 400 live welfare projects on healthcare, and empowerment, in over 2000 remote villages and slums across 25 states of India.

Serving the elderly in our society
who are in need of medical attention is the sole mission of give a life charitiy.
We believe in working towards the betterment of the elderly patients
and creating an infrastructure to empower every older person.
Our mission is taking care of their physical and mental health.

Our Reach

In keeping with its philosophy of 'Giving A life to oldage', Give A Life, our charity is associate in Delhi, for supporting the needy old once, has taken its intervention into the interiors of India, reaching the unreached in the remotest of rural areas and urban slums with our services and making this helping charity in India, the best charity in India for old once.
